Visa Debit Cards

Metamora State Bank Visa Debit Cards works like a check anywhere you see the Visa Card symbol. The transaction amount will be deducted from your checking account and will show up on your monthly statement. The Visa Card can also be used as an ATM Card. If your Visa Debit Card is lost or stolen after business hours call 1-800-264-5578.

Visa Gold and Visa Classic Credit Cards

Metamora State Bank offers both cards with low interest rates and no annual fee. Bill payments can be accepted right at Metamora State Bank. If your Visa Gold or Visa Classic Credit Card is lost or stolen after business hours call 1-800-876-9119.

Visa TravelMoney Cards

Metamora State Bank offers Visa TravelMoney cards that are accepted at Visa merchants and ATMs worldwide. Please see a bank representative for terms and conditions.

Gift Cards

Gift cards may be purchased in amounts of $25 to $500. Please see a bank representative for terms and conditions.

Lost or Stolen Cards

Call 800-264-5578 — If your Visa Debit Card is lost or stolen after banking business hours.
Call 800-876-9119 — If your Visa Credit Card is lost or stolen after banking business hours.
Call 866-208-3282 — If your Visa Travel Money Card or Gift Card is lost or stolen after banking business hours.
