BusinessPlus Checking

Our BusinessPlus Checking account does not receive a monthly maintenance fee as long as a minimum daily balance of $500.00 is maintained per month. The minimum balance to open the BusinessPlus Checking is $50.00. A charge of $30.00 will be applied to an account closed within six months of opening.

  • No monthly maintenance fee if a minimum daily balance of $500.00 is maintained
  • Monthly maintenance fee is $10.00 if minimum balance is not met
  • First 200 checks written free
  • Over 200 checks, 25¢ per check
  • Checks held in safekeeping
  • Six copies of checks free per year - $2.00 per check after six
  • Monthly statement
  • Internet Banking Access

Business Checking

Our low-cost Business Checking account is one of the best business values around. Unlike many banks, we don't charge for each deposit ticket or each item you deposit, and our simplified monthly fee plus per-check pricing makes it easy to keep track of your account costs. The minimum balance to open a Business Checking account is $50.00. A charge of $30.00 will be applied to an account closed within six months of opening.

  • Monthly maintenance fee, $10.00
  • Each check paid, 25¢
  • A monthly earnings credit generated on your average monthly balance is used to reduce or eliminate monthly service fees. This earnings credit rate is at management's discretion and may change anytime.

NOW Sole Proprietorship or a Non-Profit Checking

If your business is a Sole Proprietorship or a Non-Profit organization, you can earn a tiered interest rate on your checking with our NOW Sole Proprietorship/Non-Profit account. The minimum balance to open a NOW Sole Proprietorship/Non-Profit Checking account is $50.00. A charge of $30.00 will be applied to an account closed within six months of opening. These accounts pay interest as described on the "Rate Disclosure" page. This is an interest-bearing account. Interest earned is used to offset monthly fees.

  • Each check paid, 25¢
